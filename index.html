About us

AT (indian institute of accountTechnology)(IIAT) is a
leading training center for global IT industry standards.
The organization was set up in 2007 to help the nascent
IT industry overcome its manpower resource challenges.
It has today grown to be a prominent service provider in
talent development initiatives for the ever-evolving needs
of the IT industry. Enterprises across the world are using
the IIAT training model as a strategic tool to impart high
-quality,employment-oriented education to students. The
organization produces and shares the best learning practices
and ensures quality.
